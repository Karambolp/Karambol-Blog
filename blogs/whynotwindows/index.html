Why You Should Not Use Windows.

19 April 2024

Hello everyone, I hope your day is going well.
Today's topic is windows , good reading.

Price: Windows has always been ruthless in pricing,
for example someone who wants to buy windows 11 pro
has to pay 200 dollars and also has to pay for programs
like microsoft office because they are optimized in windows.

Privacy: Windows has never cared about the privacy of its
users, it has always collected our data, officially and unofficially,
and we must remember that our personal information is more
important than companies, we do not pay microsoft to steal our data.

Customizing: Windows is not a customizable enough system,
it lags far behind bsd and linux in this regard. Think of it like this, you
bought a computer but you can't even delete the edge application because
it has windows in it, or you have to use third party applications to customize
your desktop because the customization options that windows offers you do
not go beyond changing the wallpaper.

Performance: Windows tires your computer even if you don't do anything,
it consumes a lot of power because there are unnecessary applications running
in the background for windows, for example edge is running in the background
even if you don't use the edge browser.
